<h1 id="fx-demos-top">FX Shortcodes — samples &amp; attribute coverage</h1>

Legacy <code>&#91;sticky&#93;</code> shortcode is listed at the end.</p>

<h2 id="fx-common">Common attributes</h2>
<p>Usable on almost every <code>&#91;element type="…"&#93;</code> wrapper: <code>class</code>, <code>id</code>, <code>tag</code> (allowed: div, section, article, aside, header, footer, main, nav, figure), <code>width</code> (<code>full</code> → alignfull, <code>wide</code> → alignwide, or any CSS length), <code>align</code> (left | center | right), <code>vertical-align</code> (top | center | bottom), <code>background</code> (image URL), <code>background-color</code>, <code>color</code> or <code>text</code> (same effect), <code>padding</code>, <code>margin</code>, <code>gap</code>, <code>border-radius</code> or <code>radius</code>, <code>shadow</code> (box-shadow), <code>min-height</code>, <code>height</code> (often maps to min-height on cover), <code>max-width</code>.</p>

<h2 id="fx-columns">Columns / column</h2>
<p><strong>On <code>columns</code>:</strong> <code>columns</code> (count), <code>stack</code> (<code>1</code> collapse on narrow / <code>0</code> keep row), <code>gap</code>, <code>width</code>, <code>vertical-align</code>, common styles.</p>
